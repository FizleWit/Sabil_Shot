Sabil Shot was designed by Amine Sabil as a Capstone Project in 2023. 
It was designed with Tauri and Rust. Its purpose is to create recordings and cache the screen incase you ever want to record something that just happened. To record desktop audio you must have a Stereo Mix device setup and enabled and install FFMPEG and add it to the your path. 

The capturing of audio and video devices is handled through FFMPEG. Currently this app is only supported for Windows.  Plans, known bugs, and feature requests can all be made on the GitHub page. To use a specific device you must know the devices name as FFMPEG knows it. The easiest way to get it is with a list_devices command. More information is available on the GitHub page.
